Currently, the **Caesar Cipher** is available. More ciphers will be added in future updates!

Caesar Cipher:

The Caesar cipher is one of the simplest and most widely known encryption techniques. It's a type of substitution cipher in which each letter in the plaintext is replaced by a letter some fixed number of positions down the alphabet. For example, with a left shift of 3, D would be replaced by A, E would become B, and so on.
